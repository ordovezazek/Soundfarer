The website contains a progress
tracker is meant to help artists that
strive to become more involved with
the local music scene.

The tasks provided will help the
users to gain an understanding of
the local music scene, to make
connections, and to grow as artists.

You need to sign up so you can start
tracking your progress!

Many artists have their own journeys in the local music scene, and their stories are personal and unique to themselves. Many established artists had gone through their own trials and troubles before making it to where they are today.

These are stories of various artists about their own experiences and insights over the course of their own journeys.

The website aims to motivate its users through testimonials from various other local artists and their experiences and to provide the necessary information and resources through a progress tracker that will help them to understand how to operate as an independent artist in the local music scene.
